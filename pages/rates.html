Services and Fees

Notarization

Each $10.00. Some documents require multiple notarizations. A valid, government issued photo ID is required for each signing.

Certified Copy

Each $20.00. I can certify a copy by either meeting you at a copy store and watching the actual copy as it is made or
if you provide the original and a color copy, we can compare the two documents and inscribe the notarial
language to certify it as a true copy of the original.

Real estate transaction

Real estate fee: $120-$150 each signing. Rate determined by amount of pages, call or email for a quote!

Travel Charge

Travel Fee: $3.00 per mile (minimum $10.00). I charge a round-trip travel fee in addition to notary fees.
I use google maps to calculate the distance between my location to yours.
